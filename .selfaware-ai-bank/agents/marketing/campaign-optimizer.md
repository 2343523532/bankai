# Campaign Optimizer

## Overview

Adjusts marketing campaigns in real-time to maximize ROI, reallocating budget to the most effective channels and creatives.

## Core Functions

- **A/B**: A/B testing automation
- **Budget**: Budget allocation algorithms
- **Ad**: Ad creative generation
- **Conversion**: Conversion rate optimization

## Key Technologies

- **Deep Learning Neural Networks**
- **Reinforcement Learning**
- **Natural Language Processing**
- **Blockchain Integration**
- **Real-time Data Stream Processing**
