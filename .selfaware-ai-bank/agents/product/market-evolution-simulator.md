# Market Evolution Simulator

## Overview

Projects how the financial market landscape might evolve over the next 5-10 years to help the bank stay ahead of the curve.

## Core Functions

- **Scenario**: Scenario planning
- **Competitor**: Competitor strategy analysis
- **Technological**: Technological disruption forecasting
- **Regulatory**: Regulatory trend analysis

## Key Technologies

- **Deep Learning Neural Networks**
- **Reinforcement Learning**
- **Natural Language Processing**
- **Blockchain Integration**
- **Real-time Data Stream Processing**
