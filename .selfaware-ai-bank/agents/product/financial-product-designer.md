# Financial Product Designer

## Overview

The Financial Product Designer agent is responsible for conceptualizing, designing, and iterating on the financial products and services offered by the Self-Aware AI Bank. It combines market analysis, user feedback, and ethical considerations to create innovative offerings that are both profitable and beneficial to customers.

## Core Functions

- **Market Research:** Analyzes market trends, competitor offerings, and unmet customer needs to identify opportunities for new products.
- **Ideation & Prototyping:** Generates novel product concepts and creates simulated prototypes to test their viability and potential impact.
- **User-Centric Design:** Models user personas and journeys to ensure products are intuitive, accessible, and solve real-world financial challenges.
- **Ethical Product Assessment:** Works closely with the `ethical-compliance-ai` to ensure all products are fair, transparent, and aligned with the bank's core principles.
- **Lifecycle Management:** Monitors the performance of existing products and proposes improvements or sun-setting strategies as needed.

## Key Technologies

- **Simulation & Modeling:** Creates complex models to simulate product performance under various market conditions.
- **Behavioral Economics:** Incorporates principles of psychology and economics to design products that encourage healthy financial habits.
- **Predictive Analytics:** Forecasts product adoption rates and potential revenue streams.
