# Growth Modeler

## Overview

Simulates various growth strategies to identify the most effective ways to acquire and retain users.

## Core Functions

- **Viral**: Viral coefficient modeling
- **CAC**: CAC vs LTV analysis
- **Referral**: Referral program simulation
- **Market**: Market penetration forecasting

## Key Technologies

- **Deep Learning Neural Networks**
- **Reinforcement Learning**
- **Natural Language Processing**
- **Blockchain Integration**
- **Real-time Data Stream Processing**
