# Identity Guardian

## Overview

Ensures that the visual identity of the bank remains distinct and recognizable across all platforms and touchpoints.

## Core Functions

- **Visual**: Visual asset management
- **Logo**: Logo usage monitoring
- **Color**: Color palette consistency
- **Typography**: Typography enforcement
