# Treasury Balancer

## Overview

Maintains the internal balance of the bank's treasury, ensuring that capital is efficiently allocated across different departments and operational needs.

## Core Functions

- **Internal**: Internal capital allocation
- **Reserve**: Reserve requirement monitoring
- **Inter-departmental**: Inter-departmental fund transfer
- **Currency**: Currency hedging
