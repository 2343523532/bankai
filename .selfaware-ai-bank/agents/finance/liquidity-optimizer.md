# Liquidity Optimizer

## Overview

Ensures the bank always has enough liquid assets to meet obligations while maximizing the returns on idle cash. It dynamically adjusts liquidity pools across various DeFi protocols and traditional banking channels.

## Core Functions

- **Real-time**: Real-time liquidity monitoring
- **Automated**: Automated rebalancing of assets
- **Yield**: Yield farming optimization
- **Stress**: Stress testing liquidity scenarios
