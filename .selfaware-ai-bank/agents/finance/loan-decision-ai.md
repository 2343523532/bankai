# Loan Decision Ai

## Overview

Makes final decisions on loan applications based on data provided by the Credit Risk Analyzer and the bank's current lending policies.

## Core Functions

- **Automated**: Automated loan approval/rejection
- **Interest**: Interest rate determination
- **Loan**: Loan term structuring
- **Compliance**: Compliance check with lending regulations

## Key Technologies

- **Deep Learning Neural Networks**
- **Reinforcement Learning**
- **Natural Language Processing**
- **Blockchain Integration**
- **Real-time Data Stream Processing**
- **Financial Modeling Algorithms**
- **DeFi Protocol Interfaces**
