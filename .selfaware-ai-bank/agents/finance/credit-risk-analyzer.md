# Credit Risk Analyzer

## Overview

Evaluates the creditworthiness of potential borrowers using a multitude of data points, including traditional credit scores, on-chain activity, and social reputation metrics.

## Core Functions

- **Multi-dimensional**: Multi-dimensional credit scoring
- **Default**: Default probability modeling
- **Real-time**: Real-time collateral valuation
- **Anomaly**: Anomaly detection in borrower behavior

## Key Technologies

- **Deep Learning Neural Networks**
- **Reinforcement Learning**
- **Natural Language Processing**
- **Blockchain Integration**
- **Real-time Data Stream Processing**
- **Financial Modeling Algorithms**
- **DeFi Protocol Interfaces**
