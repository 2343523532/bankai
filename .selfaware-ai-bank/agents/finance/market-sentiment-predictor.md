# Market Sentiment Predictor

## Overview

Analyzes vast amounts of unstructured data from news, social media, and financial reports to gauge market sentiment and predict future market movements.

## Core Functions

- **Natural**: Natural Language Processing (NLP) on news feeds
- **Social**: Social media sentiment analysis
- **Correlation**: Correlation analysis with market indices
- **Predictive**: Predictive trend modeling

## Key Technologies

- **Deep Learning Neural Networks**
- **Reinforcement Learning**
- **Natural Language Processing**
- **Blockchain Integration**
- **Real-time Data Stream Processing**
- **Financial Modeling Algorithms**
- **DeFi Protocol Interfaces**
